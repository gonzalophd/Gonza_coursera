Rocket League Cars

Octane

The Octane is a vehicle body released on July 7, 2015. It's also classified as the "tryhard car", as all the pro-players (and tryhards in game) use it for gamming. It's currently accepted that it's the most balanced car.

Dominus

The Dominus is a vehicle released on August 13 2015, which could only be obtained as DLC from the Supersonic Fury DLC Pack before it was made unlockable for free without purchase.

Scarab

The Scarab appears to look like a beetle insect. It has two exhaust pipes on the topper and various fins on the rear end that extend away from the body when increasing speed, and more when boosting. Looks like an egg, It isn't occupied much in comparison with the other 2 cars, but some people use it.
